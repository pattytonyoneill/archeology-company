Services Provided:

Test Trenching

Test trenching will be performed via digger or excavator. This will be done to look at signifigant areas of interest where archeology may be present based off of the surrounding monuments and sites. We will do this to determine general site straitigraphy. Test trench length will vary depend on the site size & number of sites to be tested.

  • Cost per Day - price will be agreed upon when geophysical surveys have been completed
  • Cost per Hour - starts off at a basic rate of $$$.
  • Discounts Offered - if there multiple sites, site size, and soil conditions on site.

Site Evaluation

Our team will do research before coming to the site. The research includes looking at the geological conditions, historical maps, and nearby archaeological studies. When arriving on site samples are taken, notes on nearby flora & fauna, and building structures. Conditions of the site may increase the overall cost of the excavation. Total cost will be discussed after the site evalutation takes place. This process can take a number of days to a number of weeks or months depending on scale. Inital costing will cover fees in relation to this service.

  • Cost per Day - $$$
  • Cost per Hour - $$$
  • Discounts Offered - $$$

Geophysical Survey

This will be preformed by a geophysical specialist and our team in order to survey the landscape and overlying archaeologicl systems. After being completed we will inform you of the results of the geophysical survey and where the test trenches will be placed. We will in a few days to a weeks time get back to you to inform you of the cost.

  • Metal detecting is illegal in the Republic of Ireland unless by a licensed professional.

Digital Archeology

When a digital survey takes place we will be recording ariel footage of the site, compiling the site onto a 3D map, and also possibly be running geophysical survey scans. However, on the off chance you come to us to run a 3D imaging of a specific item and feature we can also create a 3D model for you of the item or feature. We can also try to build you a replica of what the monument may have looked like.

  • Drone Scanner/Imaging
  • 3D Imagining of Site
  • 3D Imaging of Artefacts
  • 3D Printing
  • 3D Imaging from Scratch
